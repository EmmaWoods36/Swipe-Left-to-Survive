# Game Design Skeleton

## Core idea

Amy fights dating chaos as literal combat.

Main game:
- Conversation choices trigger moves.
- CPU/enemy response is turn-based.
- Battles represent emotional/social situations.

Arcade mode:
- Full chaotic fighting roster.
- Same characters and sprites.
- Not necessarily canon; it is the bonus "metaphor becomes literal" mode.

## Roster categories

### Amy
Main character. Balanced fighter.

Possible moves:
- Pink Energy Blast
- Low Kick
- High Kick
- Boundary Guard
- Group Chat Intervention
- Red Flag Rejection

### Friends
Support fighters who can also do emotional/social damage.

Possible moves:
- Hard Truth Beam
- Receipts Drop
- Group Chat Intervention
- Screenshot Evidence
- Girl Stand Up

### Good boys
Support/healer/cleanse characters.

They should not be primarily damage dealers.

Possible moves:
- Consistency Heal
- Clear Communication
- Respectful Distance
- Green Flag Shield
- Plans Made Three Days in Advance

### Red flag guys
Standard enemies and possible arcade joke fighters.

Possible archetypes:
- Love Bomber
- Breadcrumb Guy
- Situationship Sorcerer
- Gaslight Goblin
- Future Faker
- Hot and Cold Menace

### Concept bosses
Big metaphor bosses.

Current:
- Dating App Algorithm
- The Pattern of Letting Toxic Men In

## Health bar naming options

Instead of HP, use:
- Resolve
- Peace
- Emotional Energy
- Self-Respect
- Delusion Meter for enemies
- Toxicity Meter for villains

Current skeleton uses **Resolve**.

## Turn timer

Because the game is turn-based but chaotic, every battle turn should have a timer.

Current prototype:

- 15 seconds per Amy turn
- Visible countdown bar
- Last 5 seconds pulse as danger
- Timeout triggers Boundary Guard by default

Possible later variants:

- Easy: 25 seconds
- Normal: 15 seconds
- Hard: 10 seconds
- Extreme: 5 seconds
- Boss fights can reduce timer when Amy is glitched, distracted, confused, or haunted.

Status interaction ideas:

- Distracted: -3 seconds
- Glitched: buttons shuffle or flicker
- Confused: one option is replaced with "Text him back"
- Haunted: Pattern enemy gains power if the timer expires
